Affirm Savings 2018–2019

 
 
 
 
 
 
About
 
 
 
Affirm’s mission is to deliver honest financial products that improve lives. As part of making good on that mission the company looked to diversify away from its core lending product. Its latest offering is a no-fee high-yield savings account.
 
I had the opportunity to work on the design of the savings pilot and later, work alongside my colleague Andres Limcaoco on the first new feature of the account—savings goals.

Affirm Experiments 2018

 
 
 
 
 
 
About
 
 
 
I had the pleasure of working with a team of highly skilled engineers to bring to life axp— an A/B testing platform that would become a crucial tool in Affirm’s product development process.
 
The goal of this project was to provide objectivity to product decisions but also to encourage a culture of experimentation—start thinking about product development and specifically design as a continuous series of tweaks rather than stepped redesign model.
